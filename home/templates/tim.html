Timothy Stelmat

Electrical Engineer

University of Iowa

I am studying Electrical Engineering at the University of Iowa and currently hold an associates in Electronics Systems Technology from the Community College of the Air Force. Before transferring to the University of Iowa, he did volunteer work on a CubeSAT to be launched into space by NASA. Upon transferring, he has worked in the Department of Neuroscience and Pharmacology, working on embedded systems and optical projects for the lab. This ranges from creating a controller that helps with learning and memory experiments to creating a light sealed box with RGB and IR LED functionality at specific wavelengths for Optogenetic experiments. Additionally, he has been working with Dr. Jui-kai (Ray) Wang as a volunteer, learning about VAE deep learning models in order to help Dr. Wang in the future with Ophthalmology research using these models, which will help Ophthalmologists better treat their patients. All this experience has helped him gain knowledge of C, C++, Java, Python, hardware design, optical components, PCB layout, schematic design, deep learning, and embedded systems.
